If your vehicle does not start after following the factory wiring and assembly standards, submit a support ticket below.

Submit a Diagnostic Ticket

Pre-Diagnostic Checklist

Ensure the vehicle is supplied with these items before starting:

  • Air: Check Carburettor.
  • Fuel: Fuel being provided and pumped to vehicle.
  • Spark: Sparkplugs seated and tightened correctly.
  • Compression: Make sure Block is intact.
  • Timing: Make sure timing is correct.
  • Wiring: Make sure vehicle is correctly wired to Factory standards.
