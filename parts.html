Tuning Procedures

Wheel Alignment

  1. Take a Size 15 Wrench.
  2. Scroll the way the wheel is misaligned until it doesn’t move anymore.
  3. Scroll exactly 60 times the opposite way to ideally align the wheels.

Note: Do not align wheels immediately after suspension assembly as it may bug. Save the game first.

↑ Back to Parts List

Rocker Tuning

  1. Tighten each rocker properly twice with a screwdriver.
  2. With a Size 12 Wrench, scroll all the way up on the rocker until the nut stops moving.
  3. Scroll the opposite way 58 times.
↑ Back to Parts List

Carburettor Tuning

Use a Screwdriver while the engine is running.

  • Performance Target: ~15.0 AFR.
  • Inspection Standard: 14.0 - 15.0 AFR at idle.
  • Racing Carb: Scroll up 44 times on all 4 screws, then down 10 times.
↑ Back to Parts List

Distributor Tuning

  1. Screw distributor fully in to start the engine, then unscrew by 1 click.
  2. If you hear a high-pitched chirping (too advanced), scroll down until it stops.
  3. Scroll up until chirping begins again.
  4. Set distributor to 1 position away from the chirping sound.
